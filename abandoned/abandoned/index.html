this place was never meant to be found

but you are here

the lights are still on

no one remembers why


hover slowly

the hallway keeps its lights on
as if someone is still expected to return

this room has forgotten its purpose
only the echo remains

time moves differently here
nothing rushes to leave

you feel like you've been here before
but never long enough to remember why

the silence is not empty
it is watching

every door promises meaning
none of them keep it

this place does not miss you
but it notices when you are gone

loneliness settles here
like dust on unused furniture

you were never invited
yet the room made space for you

nothing happens here
and that is the point

nothing ends here

it just stays
